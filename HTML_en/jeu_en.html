Synopsis

Since the beginning of time, the World-Tree has guaranteed life. It gathers 3 ancient worlds in its branches and allows to travel between them.

However, Loki, the god of discord in the Nordic world, has stolen the Heartstone, which is the energy reserve of the World-Tree. Cunningly, he broke the stone into 3 pieces, which he scattered in each of the kingdoms. He is convinced that no one will ever be able to survive the crossing of all the worlds and thus reunite the pieces of the Heartstone again.

Without the Heartstone, the tree has no more energy and dies little by little. It is urgent to act and reconstitute the heart of the tree !

As the Guardian of the World-Tree, protecting the life that the tree shelters is your mission. It is your duty to gather these stones. You are the last hope of this world.
